Human Resource Development

Message from the Chief Human Resource Officer,

Corporate Relations & Industry Partnerships | Placements & Career Support (C&P) | Learning & Development (L&D) | Industry-Institute Innovation Council (IIIC) | Campus Recruitment Training (CRT) | Centre for Life Skills | Consultancy Services | Corporate Centre.
“At the Department of HRD, we empower students and faculty with industry-relevant skills to thrive in a dynamic professional landscape. Our commitment to growth is driven by enduring industry partnerships and a future-forward vision that ensures our community remains agile, competent, and impactful.”
 

Empowering People. Strengthening Institutions. Building Futures.

At Sapthagiri NPS University, we believe that the strength of an institution lies in the strength of its people. The Human Resource Development (HRD) Department serves as the backbone of our university ecosystem by nurturing talent, promoting excellence, and creating a culture of continuous growth.
The HRD Department is committed to building a vibrant academic environment by attracting, developing, and retaining highly qualified faculty, administrative professionals, and support staff who contribute meaningfully to teaching, research, innovation, and student success.
 

Prof. Gurucharan Singh

CHRO

Core Values

The Human Resource Development (HRD) Department at Sapthagiri NPS University is responsible for strategic workforce planning, recruitment, faculty development, employee engagement, performance management, and institutional growth initiatives.

Our Vision

  • To attract and retain highly competent academic and administrative professionals.
  • To foster a culture of continuous learning and professional development.
  • To ensure transparent, fair, and ethical HR practices.
  • To promote diversity, equity, and inclusion across the university.
  • To align human resource strategies with institutional growth goals.

Our Mission

To create a dynamic, inclusive, and performance-driven workforce that contributes to academic excellence, research innovation, and institutional leadership at national and global levels.
